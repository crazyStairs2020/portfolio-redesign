Enhancing accessibility on Providence.gov








    




          Government websites may be one of the most important interfaces
          where accessibility is essential

          With the help of tools such as WAVE and Lighthouse by Google,
          recommendations for improvements could be found for a wide
          range of features.

          This site redesign intends to focus purely on fixing these problems
          and not recreate the site as a whole. Therefore the new rendition
          appears very minimalist yet directly addresses some of the issues raised.

Summary

      
 
          This redesign addresses one of many very important interfaces that contain
          accessibility related issues that could result in community participation being
          more difficult for people with certain needs. Government websites are especially
          important in relation to these design concepts since they may be used for voter
          registration, taxes, filing complaints, and many other crucial parts of community involvement
